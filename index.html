Biography (CV)

I am currently a final-year M.S. candidate with School of Electronic and Computer Engineering, Peking University. Before that, I graduated from School of Electronic and Information Engineering, South China University of Technology with the highest undergraduate honor (only 10 awardees in the entire university per year).

 

My research interest mainly lies in statistical machine learning, representation learning and their applications to clustering, regression, and classification. Currently, I'm working on the robust regression and classification with non-trivial performance guarantees, discriminative dictionary/subspace learning, and robust deep ConvNet model that handles big label-noisy training data. I am so lucky to work closely with Zhiding Yu, Yandong Wen, Prof. Meng Yang and Prof. Yuexian Zou. I am co-advised by Prof. Meng Yang and Prof. Yuexian Zou.
